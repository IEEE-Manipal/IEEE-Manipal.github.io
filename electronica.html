    Interested in building electronic circuits and cracking encrypted messages?
    Here's an opportunity to apply your Electronics and Electrical skills and win all the glory!

    Krieg's Electronics & Electrical Category, ELECTRONICA consists of the following events:
    1. Circuit Racer: A two-round competition testing one's aptitude towards the application of general components to form complex circuits. Th...e initial round is written test based on general aptitude in Electronics. Selected teams make it to the second round where contestants will be practically implementing electronic circuits based on the given problem statement
    Team Size: 1
    Date: 28th March (Round 1)
    29th March (Round 2)
    Time: 5.45 PM
    Venue: AB5- 408

    2. Enigma: A single-round competition, preceded by a Cryptography Workshop on Basic Cipher Techniques. In the competition, teams must encrypt/decrypt messages employing any of the techniques taught, through an Arduino.
    Team Size: 2 (Students registering individually will be paired randomly)
    Date: 29th March (Workshop)
    30th March (Test)
    Time: 5.45 PM
    Venue: AB5- 410

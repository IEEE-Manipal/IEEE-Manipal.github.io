About IEEE

Student Branch Manipal

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. IEEE and its members inspire a global community to innovate for a better tomorrow through its highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities. IEEE is the trusted “voice” for engineering, computing, and technology information around the globe. There are more than 420,000 IEEE members in over 160 countries. IEEE publishes a third of the world’s technical literature in electrical engineering, computer science, and electronics and is a leading developer of international standards that underpin many of today's telecommunications, information technology, and power-generation products and services.

Benefits of having an IEEE Membership

IEEE members can access information on local events and activities by signing in to  myIEEE , the members' personalized gateway to IEEE membership.
In addition, members can also:

  • 1. Access individual Society memberships and subscriptions.
  • 2. Connect with local IEEE Sections and volunteer leadership.
  • 3. Find upcoming conferences.
  • 4. Learn more about individual benefits.
  • 5. Read the latest news from IEEE, IEEE Spectrum, IEEE Standards News, and The Institute.


IEEE Xplore

The IEEE Xplore digital library is a powerful resource for discovery of and access to scientific and technical content published by the IEEE and its publishing partners. The content in IEEE Xplore comprises:

  • 1. 170+ journals.
  • 2. 1,400+ conference proceedings.
  • 3. 5,100+ technical standards.
  • 4. Approximately 2,000 eBooks.
  • 5. 400+ educational courses.

Computex

28th - 30th March 2017

Computex

Interested in Programming and/or Cryptography?
Here's an opportunity to test your skills against the best coders of the college!
Krieg's Computer Science Category, COMPUTEX consists of the following events:
1. Turing: A single-round test of your Cryptographic skills in data encryption/decryption and knowledge about various ciphers.
2. Battleground: A two-round, competitive coding competition to test your analytical and algorithmic based programming skills.
The initial round involves a written test based on aptitude and algorithms. Selected teams make it to the second round which is an attempt to simulate a War where contestants find themselves in the middle of the Battleground!
Team Size: 2 (Students registering individually will be paired randomly)
(Bring fully charged laptops)

Paper Presentation

Interested in pursuing Research? Do you wish to see your hard work published, for the world to see?
Here's an opportunity to present your idea that can change the World!
Krieg's Flagship Event this year is Paper Presentation.
Details:

Electronica

28th - 30th March 2017

Electronica

Interested in building electronic circuits and cracking encrypted messages?
Here's an opportunity to apply your Electronics and Electrical skills and win all the glory!

Krieg's Electronics & Electrical Category, ELECTRONICA consists of the following events:
